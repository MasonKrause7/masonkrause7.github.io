Membership

The Society is a non-profit organization affiliated with the APSU Foundation. Membership is open, upon payment of dues, to any person, business, or organization that shares in the purpose of The Society. Business memberships are available at the Patron level and above.

How to Join

To join the Woodward Library Society or renew your membership, simply select your payment method and follow the instructions.

  • Visa/Mastercard
  • Check
  • Payroll Deduction (APSU Employees Only)

Annual Membership Levels

Current Student $5 Includes the Woodward Society's newsletter and invitations to attend events and programs.
Friend $30-$49
Family $50-$99
Contributing $100-$299 Includes the Society's newsletter, invitations to attend events and programs, and three bookplates in your honor placed in new library books.
Patron $300-$499 Includes two tickets to the Society's annual benefit event, the Society's newsletter, invitations to attend quarterly programs, and three bookplates in your honor placed in new library books.
Sponsor $500-$999 Includes three tickets to the Society's annual benefit event, the Society's newsletter, invitations to attend events and programs, and three bookplates in your honor placed in new library books.
Benefactor $1000+ Includes four tickets to the Society's annual benefit event, the Society's newsletter, invitations to attend events and programs, and three bookplates in your honor placed in new library books.
